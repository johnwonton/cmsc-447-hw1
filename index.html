Back in high school, I think it was Junior year? A few friends of mine were starting a band and they were looking for a drummer.

At that point, I had an old drum set in my grandparent's house that I barely touched, but I figured I'd be up to the task to be their new drummer.

After playing all throughout high school, participating in some school-hosted music events, getting kicked out of a BBQ place for playing too loud, and eventually getting my own electric drum kit, drumming is something I find myself doing multiple times a week!

Sometimes, in my free time and if inspiration strikes, I'll end up thinking about what it would sound like if two (usually very different) songs sound like if I put them together?

I've got a few mashups I've uploaded onto Soundcloud that are a little bit popular, one has around ~11,000 plays and the other has ~9,000 plays.

I'm just glad people like them that much!
